

# Clients

# Example Projects:

**[AstraZeneca][1] **- Collaboration and knowledge-sharing framework for the 
research & development division of this pharmaceutical company.

**[CIGNA][2]** - Informal learning and working smarter strategy for CIGNA University 
to support this health services company.

**[Canadian International Development Agency][3]** – Informal learning strategy 
and action plan for the Continuous Learning Division of this government agency. 

**[CAE][4]** – Training needs analysis and methodology development for construction 
of an aircraft virtual maintenance trainer for this engineering and professional 
services company.

**[Canadian Army][5]** – Analyzed all current training leading to a strategic 
plan for distributed training in support of the land force reserve restructuring 
project.

**[Canada School of Public Service][6]** – Identification of suppliers, staff 
training and support for an online collaborative learning environment for senior 
public servants on the_ Advanced Leadership Program_.

**[Council of Ministers of Education, Canada][7]** – Development of a unified 
strategy paper for all stakeholders for the _Pan-Canadian Online Learning Portal_. 

**[Canadian Tourism Human Resource Council][8]** – Evaluated the existing learning 
management system and specified requirements for a replacement, including writing 
of RFP and selection criteria.
